SDOF

A simple tool for studying Single Degree of Freedom systems

Hello there! My name is Jeremy Atkinson and I created SDOF as a convenient way of obtaining the response of SDOF systems. If you have any comments, suggestions, bugs, or if you just really like the tool, you can let me know at jchatkinson@gmail.com

SDOF uses recurrence formulas to calculate the system response. The method is rather straightforward, and detailed explanation is available in most structural analysis textbooks.

SDOF was built with Foundation for Apps, AngularJS, and makes use of several third-part libraries for plotting, including d3.js, n3-line-chart.js, and fft.js.
